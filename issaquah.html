About This Location

Crafted with intention. Served with care.

Opened in 2016, our Issaquah location quickly became a neighborhood favorite. The warm, homey dining room is the perfect setting for a weeknight dinner, a quick lunch, or a weekend feast with friends.

Whether you're craving butter chicken, freshly baked garlic naan, or a hearty biryani, every dish is made with the same care and recipes that have defined Naan N Curry for over two decades.
