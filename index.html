Our Story

Withstanding the test of time by offering authenticity, consistency, and reliability

"This is not just a business, but our livelihood. We stand at the intersection of hospitality, culture, and community."

In 2005, Naan N Curry opened its doors in Renton with one goal: to serve authentic Halal Pakistani and North Indian food rooted in family recipes.

Two decades and two locations later, that purpose remains unchanged. As our family has grown, so has our business, shaped by a team and a community that have embraced us every step of the way.

That same sense of responsibility led to the creation of The Shared Plate, a way for us to give back by fighting child food insecurity and caring for the communities that have cared for us.
